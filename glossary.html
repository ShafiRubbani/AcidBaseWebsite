Bonding

Two substances bond when they react and share/exchange electrons.

Concentration

Concentration is the measure of the density of a particular substance in a solution.

Covalent

A bond is said to be covalent if the two substances involved share electrons.

Dissociate

A molecule dissociates when it breaks down into smaller molecules and/or atoms.

Distillation

Distillation is a process in which liquids are separated from impurities through boiling.

Electrolyte

An electrolyte is a solution that can produce ions to conduct electricity.

Ion

An ion is an atom or molecule that is charged through the gain/loss of electrons.

Metal Oxide

A metal oxide is a compound consisting of a metal and oxygen.

Non-metal Oxide

A nonmetal oxide is a compound consisting of a nonmetal and oxygen.

Proton

A proton is a positively charged subatomic particle found in the nucleus of atoms. It is also a hydrogen ion.

Reaction

A interaction between substances that creates substances of new properties.

Soluble

A solid is said to be soluble in a liquid if it can be dissolved in said liquid.

Theory

A scientific statement supported by evidence and logic.
